## 📖 Understanding the Package

### The Dragon's Journey

1. **Before Event (Optional):** Dragons browse `dragon-complete-package.html` online to familiarise themselves
2. **On Arrival:** Dragons receive printed `The_Den_Master_Print_Pack.docx`
3. **During Event:** Dragons use `The_Den_Dragon_Scoresheet.xlsx` to score
4. **After Event:** Feedback is compiled from all scoresheets

### The 5 Evaluation Dimensions (All Equally Weighted at 20% each)

1. **The Idea & Business** — Problem clarity, differentiation, pitch quality
2. **Progress & Traction** — Customers, revenue, partnerships, learning from challenges
3. **Impact & Responsibility** — Social impact, sustainability, genuine commitment
4. **Support & Vision** — Clear needs, credible 12-month plan, strategic thinking
5. **The Founder** — Credibility, passion, communication, coachability

### The Scoring Scale

- **5 – Excellent:** Compelling, well-evidenced, innovative
- **4 – Strong:** Well explained, supported by data
- **3 – Solid:** Clear, credible, with some evidence (respectable score)
- **2 – Developing:** Some potential but lacks depth
- **1 – Weak:** Vague, unclear, significant gaps
- **0 – No Answer/Not Relevant:** Doesn't address dimension
