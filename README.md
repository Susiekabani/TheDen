## ✅ Scoring System

### Five Equally Weighted Dimensions (20% each)

1. **The Idea & Business** — Clarity, problem validation, differentiation
2. **Progress & Traction** — Evidence, resilience, learning
3. **Impact & Responsibility** — Social benefit, sustainability
4. **Support & Vision** — Strategic needs, 12-month growth plan
5. **The Founder** — Credibility, passion, communication

### Scoring Scale (0–5)

| Score | Level | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **5** | Outstanding | Exceptional on this dimension |
| **4** | Strong | Very good, minor gaps |
| **3** | Solid | Good fundamentals, credible |
| **2** | Developing | Some promise, lacks clarity/evidence |
| **1** | Early | Significant gaps |

**Important:** A score of 3 is solid and respectable. It does not indicate weakness.

---

## 🎓 Key Principles for Dragons

- **These finalists were already vetted** by independent judges using the same 5 dimensions
- **Your scores reflect live performance** — How they handle pressure, communicate, respond to challenges
- **All dimensions matter equally** — Don't weight one more heavily
- **Early-stage is not a penalty** — An exceptional idea-stage founder can score higher than a revenue-stage founder lacking vision
- **Be fair, consistent, and evidence-based** — Score what you see/hear, not assumptions
- **Your average across 5 dimensions = that founder's overall score**
